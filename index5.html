ABOUT US

  Puma SE is a German multinational corporation that designs and manufactures athletic and casual footwear,
 apparel and accessories, headquartered in Herzogenaurach, Bavaria, Germany. Puma is the third largest 
sportswear manufacturer in the world. The company was founded in 1948 by Rudolf Dassler (1898–1974). In 1924, 
Rudolf and his brother Adolf "Adi" Dassler had jointly formed the company Gebrüder Dassler
 Schuhfabrik ('Dassler Brothers Shoe Factory'). The relationship between the two brothers
 deteriorated until they agreed to split in 1948, forming two separate entities, Adidas and Puma.Readmore
